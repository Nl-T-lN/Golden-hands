About Golden Hands

Empowering India's elderly to earn with dignity and joy.

Golden Hands is a community initiative that helps senior citizens across India earn a livelihood by sharing their homemade pickles, snacks, and crafts. Our elders may not be professional artisans, but their skills, love, and life experience shine through every product. By supporting them, you help preserve tradition, spread happiness, and bring hope to those who need it most.
